This puzzle box safe seems like a normal safe, but there are actually hidden sliders and keyholes used to open it. Here are the steps to solve it:

1. Remove steering wheel

2. Turn the top 90 degrees

3. Tilt the box so that the axle key comes out from the hole

4. Turn the notch 180 degrees

5. Slide the slider that was behind the notch

6. Push the axle key into the revealed hole to get the money

Number of pieces: 78

Cost of pieces: $10.81
